UX Presentation

Why UX is important.

Generally speaking, it doesn’t matter how an application looks like if people don’t know how to use it.

Worse!

People do not understand what the product is about even it has a great idea.

Really bad:

Even if they get paid for using the product, they may prefer something else eventually just because of a bad UX.

What qualities should a product have for company survival?

Firstly, a product should be attractive to investors. Of course, it based mainly on trust.

Many components are involved in building trust:
- great idea;
- great communications;
- (and many things honestly I don’t know).

Do you trust?

ex. Apple





Do you trust?

YES!

(even if they don't use Apple products)

Why?

Because one’s sure that once they interact with the company, the user experience will be smooth.

Secondly, a product should be attractive to the users. It is in people’s psychology to divide everything on “bad" and “good".


Good: will buy, use, tell other people how great it is;
Bad: buy competitor's.

User experience is the leverage for switching “bad" to “good" in the user's mind.

Why I can use this leverage.

Work on UX is especially important on projects that offer innovations. Many unsolved questions on each step of the product.

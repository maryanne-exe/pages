profile

I’m 24 years old Interface designer and live in sunny city of Buenos Aires, Argentina, born in beautiful Saint Petersburg, Russia.
I believe that my work is about seeing a big picture, being empathetic and building right design process. Communicative team player and enthusiastic problem solver with endless curiosity to all aspects of the project.
I constantly learn new things - at the moment it’s UI development. Have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and during my studies I was gaining experience in software development, design and 3d graphics. Afterwards I found myself in User Experience Design. Expert in a variety of UX guidelines, platforms, and UI patterns. Worked with Apple HIG, Material Design and Windows guidelines. Familiar with web front-end development (HTML, CSS, some JavaScript) and iOS UI development in Xcode.
Currently I’m available for new challenging options for progress.
